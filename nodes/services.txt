Pods are 
- temporary
- created often
- ip changes constanly

Service provide
- stable ip
- stable dns name
- loadbalancing
- abstraction over pods

A Service is a stable networking layer that exposes Pods and load balances traffic to them.

Services does not talk directly to deployment

it taks to pods using selector.

2 way to create Service

1 - kubectl expose deploymen app1 --port=80 type=NodePort ## kubectl expose app1 --port=80 type=LoadBalancer
## kubectl expose deployment app1   --type=NodePort   --name=app1-service

What kubectl expose deployment does?
 - Kubernetes automatically creates a Service for your Deployment.

In simple terms

 - It “publishes your application inside the cluster”

More specifically:

 - It creates a stable network endpoint so Pods can be accessed.

Service acts like:
 - smart traffic router
