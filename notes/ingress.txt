Users
  ↓
https://myapp.com
  ↓
Ingress Controller
  ↓
frontend-service (ClusterIP)
  ↓
frontend Pods

frontend Pods
  ↓
backend-service (ClusterIP)
  ↓
backend Pods

backend Pods
  ↓
mysql-service (ClusterIP)
  ↓
MySQL Pod

####IMPORTANT

####HOW DOES INGRESS WORK?

Ingress Controller is basically:

nginx reverse proxy inside Kubernetes

Internet
  ↓
NGINX Ingress
  ↓
Service
  ↓
Pods

Ingress = HTTP/HTTPS Router for Kubernetes
