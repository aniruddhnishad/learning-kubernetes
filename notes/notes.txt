INTERVIEW GOLD QUESTION
Why is Ingress preferred over NodePort in production?

Because Ingress provides:

centralized routing
TLS termination
virtual hosting
path-based routing
better scalability
clean ports 80/443

Can we update ConfigMap without restarting pod?

👉 Yes (if mounted as volume)

But env vars require restart.

Why base64 in Secret?

Not security — just encoding for safe transport.

Where are secrets stored?

Inside etcd (Kubernetes database)
